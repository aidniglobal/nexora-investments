Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to common questions about Nexora and the services provided.

What does Nexora do?

Nexora is a visa and residency assistance platform that provides program comparisons, document requirements, Europass resume conversion, cover letter generation, and connects users with vetted consultants.

Can I upload my resume?

Yes — upload PDFs (max 2MB). We extract key information to create a Europass-formatted resume and validate passport-style photos when required.

How accurate are visa requirements?

Visa requirements are seeded from official-looking sources and may be updated by admins through the import tool; however, users should always verify with official government/VFS websites before applying.

How do I contact the team?

Use the Inquiry form to send questions to our support team. Admins can respond via the admin interface.

Is my data safe?

We follow best-practice storage for uploaded files and contact data. Review our Privacy Policy for details.

How can I become a consultant?

Consultants can apply through our contact channels — email us at {{ company_info.email }} with your credentials and area-of-expertise.
